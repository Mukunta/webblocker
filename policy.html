Network Usage Policy

Introduction

This Network Usage Policy outlines the guidelines and restrictions for the use of the organization's network resources.

Acceptable Use

Employees are expected to use the network resources responsibly and for legitimate business purposes only.

Prohibited activities include:

  • Accessing or distributing illegal or copyrighted materials
  • Engaging in any form of unauthorized hacking or unauthorized access to systems
  • Sending spam emails or engaging in any form of unauthorized mass communication
  • Visiting gambling and illicit websites
  • Engaging in activities that may compromise network security or violate privacy rights

Consequences of Violations

Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, including but not limited to:

  • Verbal or written warnings
  • Suspension of network privileges
  • Termination of employment
  • Legal action, if applicable

Policy Review

This policy is subject to periodic review and may be updated or modified at any time.
